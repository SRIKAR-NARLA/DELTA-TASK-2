How to Play

Defend yourself against a relentless zombie apocalypse. Set up a base to protect yourself before the invasion begins. As the game starts, zombies invade from both sides, attempting to reach the survivor. Zombies damage and destroy any blocks in their path, allowing them to advance. The survivor can move and shoot zombies to prevent them from getting close. The game ends if the survivor loses all health and is overtaken by the zombies.

  • Use A and D to move the player left and right
  • Press the spacebar to jump.
  • Use W to fly. Beware, flying deplets fuel content.
  • But no worries, it can be automatically regenerated.
  • Use the mouse pointer to shoot
  • To switch weapons, use 1,2,3
  • 1: Basic Weapon which shoots bullets as projectiles
  • 2: Randomized Weapon which shoots six bullets as projectiles
  • 3: Rocket Launcher which shoots one rocket in a linear fashion
  • 4: Deploys a tower that shoots to the left($200)
  • 5: Deploys a tower that shoots to the right($200)
  • 6: Deploys a tower that shoots to the left as well as the right($300)
  • 7: Deploys a mine which explodes on contact($50)
  • 8: Deploys a trap that slows down the zombies($25)
  • 9: Deploys a block for defensive purposes($75)
  • When the zombies hit the towers/blocks, they are pushed back due to the current running through them
  • There are 3 powerups, freeze, heal and lightning strike
